How it works

From scan to recovered
in under 2 minutes

01

Paste or connect your bank

Paste a bank statement, take a photo, or connect your account via Plaid or TrueLayer. Reclaim reads your transactions instantly — no login shared.

02

AI detects every issue

Our AI scans for duplicates, price hikes, unauthorized charges, and ghost subscriptions across 100+ merchants with verified billing patterns.

03

Review & send in one tap

Reclaim writes the recovery email pre-addressed to the merchant. Choose your tone, edit if you want, and send directly from the app via Gmail.

04

Money comes back

Merchants respond, refunds are issued. Reclaim tracks every case, auto-follows up if ignored, and logs every dollar you've recovered.
